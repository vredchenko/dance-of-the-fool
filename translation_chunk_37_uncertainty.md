## General Notes

**Dual Consciousness:** Protagonist has two personalities sharing one body:
1. Hillel (human consciousness, dominant most of the time due to genetic disorder)
2. Mycelium consciousness (takes over during strobing light exposure, offers "immortality")

**Vandlik's Plan:** She knows about the dual consciousness and wants to:
- Use strobing light to suppress Hillel and talk to the mycelium
- Learn to identify other royal chimeras
- Possibly make a deal with the mycelium for "immortality"
- Leave the planet with this knowledge

**The Gambit:** Protagonist shoots himself because:
1. If he transforms into full mycelium mode, they'll take that consciousness to Earth
2. If he dies, the mycelium will regenerate him (proven before)
3. Maybe he'll wake up in time to save Elza before they leave

**Dream Sequences Purpose:**
1. Show him how chimeras operate (Nathan's death)
2. Reveal Vera killed/kidnapped Elza (Irma arranged it)
3. Keep his consciousness occupied during regeneration
4. Test his ability to recognize chimera tells even in dreams

**Nathan Gog's Death:** The protagonist witnesses this memory to understand:
- Purple flower uses personalized fear/desire smells
- Nathan feared napalm due to terrorist attack 20 years before mission
- Chimera-Irma exploited this fear to override his caution
- Chimeras can emit chitinous weapons from their bodies
- Even trained, paranoid officers can be fooled by mycelium

**Regeneration Process:**
- Subject "dies" or is severely damaged
- Mycelium creates white cocoon (like spider silk)
- New body grown from template
- Consciousness kept in dream state during growth
- Emerges with memories intact (if consciousness remains dominant)

**Key Revelation:** Protagonist's genetic disorder (neuritis causing hand numbness) is his anchor to humanity. When numbness returns after regeneration = proof he's still himself, not fully chimera.

**Structural Note:** This chunk uses nested dream sequences and POV shifts (Nathan Gog, Vera) to convey information protagonist couldn't know otherwise. The mycelium is teaching him while rebuilding his body.

**Time Passage:** Unknown how long he was in cocoon, but he wakes in Object Two-Zero (abandoned for 60 years), suggesting significant time has passed or Vandlik's team has left.
