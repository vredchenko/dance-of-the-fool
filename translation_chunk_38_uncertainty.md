## General Notes

**Two Irmas:** Critical distinction:
- Original Irma: Preserved in cocoon, consciousness trapped, "not at the wheel"
- Chimera-Irma: Copy with full memories, controlled by mycelium, killed by severing connection

**Philosophical Debate:** Chimera-Irma's Jesus argument shows mycelium's fundamental misunderstanding:
- She thinks transcending humanity = good
- Misses that Jesus chose sacrifice, chose to remain human, chose suffering
- Free will to make wrong choices > perfect existence without choice

**Transmarginal Inhibition Plan:** This was Irma's (chimera's) strategy all along:
1. Institute new sleep schedule for everyone simultaneously
2. Wait for collective nervous system overload
3. People shut down despite "don't sleep" signals
4. Mass transformation while unconscious
5. Chimeras outnumber humans by tomorrow night

**Why Originals Are Preserved:**
- To create "thinking copies" (royal chimeras), mycelium needs ongoing connection to original human brain
- Original stays in cocoon with purple threads
- Chimera operates in world but maintains link
- Severing connection = chimera shuts down
- This is mycelium's vulnerability

**Rescue Strategy:**
1. Pilot linkor (ship never designed for atmospheric entry) down to surface
2. Use testers (very limited supply ~20) for unconscious people
3. Use "why" questions to test conscious people
4. Use dogs as backup (they can smell chimeras)
5. Take only verified humans
6. Leave planet before chimera takeover complete

**Alex's Impossible Feat:** Piloting a capital ship (designed only for space/orbit) through atmospheric entry is compared to sailing ship going on land. This shows:
- Desperation of situation
- Alex's exceptional skill
- Structural limits being tested
- High risk of total failure

**Timeline Urgency:**
- Night of transmarginal inhibition has begun
- 20% will transform by morning
- Majority will be chimeras by tomorrow evening
- Rescue must happen NOW or everyone dies/transforms

**Object Two-Zero Significance:**
- Original research complex from 60 years ago
- Where purple flower was first found
- Where Irma was transformed
- Where originals are preserved in cocoons
- Possibly location of mycelium's "brain"/central intelligence
- Must be destroyed to stop chimera creation?
