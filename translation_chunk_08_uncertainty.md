## CRITICAL PLOT DEVELOPMENT

This chunk introduces **black pollen** — the alien substance that could cure Gil's genetic disease!

### What is Black Pollen?
- Harvested from giant purple carnivorous flower-creatures
- The "flower" digests prey (death beetles) by growing roots into them
- Its stamens spray black pollen when "milked"
- Smells like strawberries to lure prey

### Medical Properties:
- **Grows millions of neurons per hour**
- Repairs muscle and bone tissue
- Increases reaction time/physical performance
- Healed Okamura's broken spine in one month (paralysis → full recovery)
- Doctors can't explain the healing

### The Problem — It's Addictive:
- Okamura has been using for a year
- Trying to quit ("clean for a week")
- Irma warns: "You can't jump off immediately!"
- Danger of quitting cold turkey after long-term use
- It's being illicitly harvested and distributed

### Gil's Dilemma:
- This substance could potentially cure his genetic disease (grows neurons!)
- But it's clearly against regulations (secret operation)
- Using it risks losing Corps insurance (his family's safety net)
- Irma calls it "humanity's greatest discovery"
- Gil refuses to try it... for now

This is the major turning point that will drive the rest of the plot.
