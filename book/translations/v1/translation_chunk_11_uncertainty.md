This chunk contains the **biggest sci-fi horror reveal** so far:

**NOT drug psychosis - ALIEN METAMORPHOSIS:**

1. **The Cocoon Discovery**

   - Made of webbing, recently torn open
   - Okamura "hatched" from it that morning

2. **Evidence of Metamorphosis**
   - Room trashed with eating binge
   - Refrigerator full of empty food containers
   - Pile of clothes reeking of excrement (shed?)

3. **Irma's Analysis**
   - "He crawled out of the cocoon and attacked the food"
