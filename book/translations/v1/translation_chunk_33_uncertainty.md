## General Notes

**Banshee chimera**: The sonic attack chimera is a new type, distinct from the doppelgangers. The mouth transformation and sonic weapon create effective body horror.

**Moral crisis**: The protagonist's discovery that Irma planned to abandon everyone is the climax. His refusal to abandon the colony children shows his character growth.

**Irma's complexity**: She's revealed as both sympathetic (cancer survivor fighting fate) and ruthless (willing to sacrifice hundreds). Her lies are protective but manipulative.

**Technical accuracy**: The lithium deuteride fusion fuel and the physics of thermonuclear weapons are accurate, adding realism to the sci-fi weaponry.

**The plan unravels**: Each detail (two-seater boat, no real pilots, broken shuttle decoy) reveals Irma's elaborate deception meant to save only herself, the protagonist, and Elza.

**Dream sequence**: The shift to Irma's POV and dream narrative is significant - suggests the mycelium/fungus network has been communicating with her, possibly guiding her to something important.
