## General Notes

**Doppelganger encounter**: The chimera taking the protagonist's form is a key psychological horror moment. Maintained the uncanny valley effect of recognizing yourself as different from how you see yourself in mirrors/videos.

**Biblical quotes**: Multiple Ecclesiastes references, some correct, some deliberately misquoted. These are thematically important (cycles of life/death, transformation). Used recognizable King James-style phrasing.

**Infrasound weapon**: The chimera's fear-inducing ability is explained as infrasound. This is scientifically plausible (infrasound causes anxiety/dread in humans).

**Sleep deprivation crisis**: The 30-minute sleep limit creates constant tension. Maintained the radio check-in routine as a rhythm element.

**Military procedure**: The tester device, security protocols, and checkpoint procedures add realism to the desperate situation.

**Emotional beats**: The reunion with Elza and developing connection with Irma balance the horror/action elements.
