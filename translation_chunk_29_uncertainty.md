- Reapers (женці) - alien predators

- Chimeras - hybrid creatures created by the alien ecosystem

**Cultural Context:** The story contains references to:
- Military training and combat experience
- Soviet/post-Soviet military culture (based on informal speech patterns and hierarchy)
- Ukrainian colloquialisms adapted to sci-fi setting

**Moral Complexity:** This chunk deals with premeditated murder, moral anguish, and impossible choices. Maintained the psychological depth and horror elements in translation.

**Horror Elements:** The body horror (Siamese twin chimera with Vira's face) and violence are explicit. Translation preserves the disturbing imagery without sanitizing.
